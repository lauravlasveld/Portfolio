MOBILE APPS

CARFY

Carfy consists of an Progressive Web App (PWA) music player connected to Spotify. Crafted with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it includes hands-free voice control for a safer driving experience.

Enjoy real-time music recommendations based on your location and current traffic conditions. Carfy also offers an offline mode, ensuring uninterrupted music playback even without an internet connection.

SHEALTHY

Shealthy is an app that aims to inspire young individuals to adopt healthier habits with a touch of humorto make it more apealling to them.

I developed this app for both iOS using Swift and Android using Kotlin. The app's motivational design encourages users to scan more products, and it includes an interactive product barcode scanning, and adding items to their shopping list.

STOLPERSTEINE

The Stolpersteine App is designed to raise awareness about the Stolpersteine stones placed in cities to honor victims.

This unique application would allow users to share photos of newly laid Stolpersteine and contribute stories about individuals.

Stolpersteine App would not only preserve historical memory but also foster community connection through the remembrance of these impactful lives.

WEB DEVELOPMENT

Dehaze

The DEHAZE website project aimed to create an online platform for singer-songwriter Sjors de Haas. Operating under the artist name DEHAZE, the website serves as a showcase for his musical talents.

Key objectives include presenting DEHAZE's journey, featuring a comprehensive discography, and incorporating interactive media elements for a captivating user experience.
The site also includes an event calendar to keep fans informed about upcoming performances and seamless social media integration for enhanced interaction.

Smelly

Smelly is an mobile web app created for a Media company in Tilburg, called LiveWall. The app shows you what is left in your fridge, and when these itims will expire. The app comes with a scanning feature that scans the receipt and stores them it's products in your virtual fridge. When something in your fridge is about to expire you will get a notification.

That is where 'Smelley' comes in. Smelley is the mascot of the app in the form of a dog. The dog is supposed to make the app more fun, and to keep the user motivated. We even thought of a few Smelley games that could be played in the app, where one can actually be played.
